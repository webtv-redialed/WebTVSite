Welcome to the 90s
When WebTV launched in 1996, it was one of the easiest ways to get on the Internet.

Due to aging technology, it was shut down in 2013. Luckily, it's now back via WebTV Redialed.


Features

Mail

With Mail, you can send messages to other WebTV Redialed users.

Favorites

With Favorites, you can save your favorite websites in a folder, then revisit them again later.

Chat

With Chat, you can talk to other people around the globe via IRC (Internet Relay Chat).

Discuss

With Discuss, you can post in many different discussion groups on a wide variety of topics.

Centers

With Centers, you can keep up to date with the latest weather and news.

Messenger

With Messenger, you can send instant messages to your buddies via Escargot.

Page Builder

With Page Builder, you can create and publish your own websites.

ProtoWeb

With WebTV Redialed's ProtoWeb proxy, you can view their large collection of
manually restored sites on your WebTV box via "proto://" URLs.

Getting Started
To get started, visit our Setup guide. You can use either a real WebTV box, or a Windows PC.
