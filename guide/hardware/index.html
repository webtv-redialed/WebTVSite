There are 3 different ways for you to connect with hardware: DreamPi, VoIP, and Landline.

DreamPi is the most reliable method, but you need to have a few different things for it to work.

VoIP isn't as reliable as DreamPi, but all you need is a VoIP adapter.

Landline is the least reliable method, but if you have a landline already you don't have to buy anything. (Note: This method will not work for Dreamcast)

DreamPi

Landline
