Features

 




WebTV Redialed allows you to surf the Internet and interact with all different types of entertainment, the fun way; on your TV!

The service acts as a replacement for the original WebTV service, which was discontinued in 2013. Utilizing zefie's minisrv project as a base, WebTV Redialed allows you to send Mail to friends, browse the web, and join a community of likeminded individuals.

 

 



WebTV Redialed is a fan project, and is in no way affiliated with Microsoft.
